Chicken Enchiladas

Description:

Enchiladas are a dish of Mexican origin consisting of corn (and rarely flour) tortillas rolled around a filling, such as: meats, beans, cheese, potatoes, or a combination. They're covered in a savory sauce of which there are a variety: chili-based sauces like salsa roja, various moles, tomatillo-based sauces like salsa verde, or cheese-based sauces such as chile con queso.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil, or as needed
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 3/4th cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1/2 pint of sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1(15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/3 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (Optional)
  • 8(10 inch) flour tortillas
  • 1(12 ounce) jar taco sauce
