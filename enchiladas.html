Chicken Enchiladas

Description:

Enchiladas are a dish of Mexican origin consisting of corn tortillas rolled around a filling, such as: meats, beans, cheese, potatoes, or a combination. They're covered in a savory sauce of which there are a variety: chili-based sauces like salsa roja, various moles, tomatillo-based sauces like salsa verde, or cheese-based sauces such as chile con queso.
